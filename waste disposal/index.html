Join the Movement to Clean Our Environment

Let's Clean is a community-driven initiative aimed at cleaning up residential areas and archaeological sites that have been neglected for years. We believe that by taking action together, we can make a significant impact on our environment and create a cleaner, healthier future for everyone.

Total 62 million tons of waste is generated per annum, and only 42 Million tons is collected.
